Interactive Map

This website contains a lightweight and interactive map feature that can help you determine detailed and accurate COVID statistics of your region.

To use it simply head click on the Map heading on the nagivational bar to be greeted with a map having all of its provinces separated by a thick border. The province you hover on is illuminated and a caption appears detailing which province is being hovered on for convenience.

After selecting your province, you will be guided to another page to select which region of the province you would like to see the statistics of. Simply select your region of choice from the dropdown menu. Regions can range from being cities, areas or in some cases, the province itself depending on the data available from the province.

Finally, you will be presented the statistics of your selected health region in a tabulated format showing only the critical data for convenience and accessibility. Additionally, the statistics page and the region selection page sport options to return to the previous page, allowing for seamless and quick navigation of the website.





Preventive Measures

This website additionally contains safety procedures for maintaining the highest possible safety amidst the pandemic. It includes 3 major tips that you can follow and shines light on what to do if you think you may have COVID.

The tips cover basic essentials that aim to make sure you are protected in the event that you may need to step outside for any reason, ranging from mandatory grocery shopping to gathering (as long as their purpose is essential)

The website also includes multiple images so that you can quickly understand the tips without having to read through all the finer print. These are meant to serve a TL;DR purpose and are directly from the World Health Organization. Moreover, the expandable sections include informative headers as well.

Finally, the data on the website has been provided by the World Health Organization, an organization striving to help reduce the impact of COVID-19 in both, developed and underdeveloped countries.





About this Project

This project was designed for CS50's Final Assignment. It includes elements of Flask, SQLite3, Python, JavaScript, HTML and CSS as demonstrated in the course.
